Animal Kingdom

Animal Kingdom is a zoological theme park and one of four parks located at the Disney World Resort. It is a unique combination of a theme park and a zoo, featuring both rides and animal exhibits.

Visit Animal Kingdom

My Experience

If you enjoy zoos, as I do, then you'll enjoy Animal Kingdom. There are animal exhibits scattered throughout the park, as well as a the Kilimanjiaro Safari ride, which has you driven through a large animal habitat filled with giraffes, flamingos, hippos, wildebeast and more. There is also a bird show, where some bird trainers show off a variety of exotic birds who have been trained at various tasks. Different parts of the park are themed after Africa and Asia, with rides that follow those themes. There is also a section based on the Avatar movies (the blue people). I'm not necessarily a fan, but the environment and the rides in that area are extremely pretty. Animal Kingdom does close earlier than the other parks for the health of the animals, so keep that in mind during the trip.

Travel

If you are staying in a Disney World Resort hotel, there are free buses that go from each hotel to each of the parks every 20 minutes or so.
