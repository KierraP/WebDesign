    • Museum of Pop Culture

    The Museum of Pop Culture, also known as MOPOP, is a pop culture museum located in the Seattle Center, near the Space Needle. It features a variety of pop culture exhibits, notably ones focused on the fantasy, sci-fi, and horror film genres. It also features exhibits about famous musicians from the Seattle area, such as Jimi Hendrix and Nirvana.

    Museum of Pop Culture

    My Experience

    There is an abundance of sculptures on display, both indoors and in an exterior garden. There is a wide variety of shapes, colors, and sizes. The scale of some of the sculptures is bigger than I ever thought blown glass could be. Overall, I enjoyed the glass exhibition and would recommend it to anyone visiting the Seattle area. There are ticket packages that give you admission to Chihuly Garden & Glass, as well as the Space Needle and/or the Museum of Pop Culture, both of which are in the vicinity.

    The Space Needle Musuem of Pop Culture

    Travel

    The best route to get to the Seattle Center is via the Seattle Monorail. The Monorail goes back and forth between the Seattle Center and Westlake Station in downtown Seattle daily between 7:30 AM to 11:00 PM. You can get to Westlake station from the SeaTac Airport by taking the Sound Transit Light Link Rail. The Light Link Rail runs from 5:00 AM to 11:00 PM daily.
