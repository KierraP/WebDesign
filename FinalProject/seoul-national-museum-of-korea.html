    • Chihuly Garden & Glass

    Chihuly Garden & Glass is an exhibition of blown glass artwork by artist Dale Chihuly. It is located in the Seattle Center, directly adjacent to the Space Needle. Chihuly is known for his large scale glass sculptures, many of which are public artworks. The Chihuly Garden & Glass features both indoor and outdoor displays of his sculptures. The building also contains a gift shop and bar.

    Chihuly Garden & Glass

    My Experience

    There is an abundance of sculptures on display, both indoors and in an exterior garden. There is a wide variety of shapes, colors, and sizes. The scale of some of the sculptures is bigger than I ever thought blown glass could be. Overall, I enjoyed the glass exhibition and would recommend it to anyone visiting the Seattle area. There are ticket packages that give you admission to Chihuly Garden & Glass, as well as the Space Needle and/or the Museum of Pop Culture, both of which are in the vicinity. I last visited in 2022, but I imagine the exhibition is much the same.

    The Space Needle Musuem of Pop Culture

    Travel

    The best route to get to the Seattle Center is via the Seattle Monorail. The Monorail goes back and forth between the Seattle Center and Westlake Station in downtown Seattle daily between 7:30 AM to 11:00 PM. You can get to Westlake station from the SeaTac Airport by taking the Sound Transit Light Link Rail. The Light Link Rail runs from 5:00 AM to 11:00 PM daily.
