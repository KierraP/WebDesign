    • National Museum of Natural History

    The National Museum of Natural History is a museum sponsored by the Smithsonian Institution. It is located on the National Mall in Washington, DC. It hosts an array of exhibits, including dinosaurs and other prehistoric fossils, an extensive rock and mineral collection, and a variety of anthropological artifacts. There is an entomology exhibit where guests can view live insects, as well as a butterfly pavilion.

    Visit Site

    The Hope Diamond is a famous jewel and a permanent display at the Museum.
